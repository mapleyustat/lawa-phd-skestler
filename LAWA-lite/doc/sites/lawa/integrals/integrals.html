==title: Integrals involving B-splines and/or wavelets==

Implementation of the calculation of integrals involving two factors.

==section: Template Parameters== The declaration of [Integral] reads ==code:.cc== template <typename T,

QuadratureType Quad, typename First, typename Second>

stru

with template parameters: ==list:begin== ->[T]: Element type of involved integrands ->[Quad]: The quadrature type to be used ->[First]: The first factor in the integrand ->[Second]: The second factor in the integrand ==list:end==

Realized are versions where at least one factor is a B-spline or B-spline wavelet.
